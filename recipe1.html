Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute, until fragrant.
  3. Add the broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes until slightly tender.
  4. Add the zucchini and continue to stir fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Pour the soy sauce and sesame oil over the vegetables, and stir to combine.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and cook for another minute.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot.

This quick and healthy vegetable stir fry is perfect as a main dish or a side! Enjoy a delicious, colorful, and nutritious meal in just minutes.
